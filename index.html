Spinning Wheelchairs

This JavaScript-powered hackathon project achieved 3rd place in the "Trust in SODA" 2021 event in conjunction with Code Institute. This rudimentary project was designed to help determine if a hallway or doorway has the adequate space to accommodate a wheelchair's wide turn radius.

  • Project Received: 3rd place in hackathon.
  • Team: of 4.
  • Time: 5 days.
  • My Role: JavaScript Algebra Implementation.
  • My Contributions: JavaScript, HTML, CSS.
  • About My Contributions: Researched the wheelchair turn algebra and implemented it in the JavaScript. Also Proposed the idea for a front-end only application. This allowed the team to streamline the app and focus on best accessibility practices over a complex design.
  • Agile Methodologies used: Constant communication was maintained through Slack and the team made use of GitHub's projects for a Kanban board. This helped us determine app features that were feasible.
